Cookie Policy

This page provides full information about the cookies we use on DocuLuma.com (hereinafter referred to as the “Site”), as well as tools and advice on how to manage them. We are constantly making improvements and changes to the way we use cookies and will regularly update this page with the latest information. Also check out our Privacy Policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are sent from websites visited by users to their devices (computers, tablets, smartphones, etc.) and stored in their browser. When you visit the same websites again, these cookies are sent back to the server.

Important: Cookies do not harm your device.

Cookie categories

1. Technical cookies

- Necessary cookies

These cookies are essential for the correct operation of the Site. They allow you to navigate pages, use features and receive requested services, such as access to restricted areas of the Site. For example, they may remember data entered when you navigate backwards or manage security tokens for various services on the Site. Without these cookies, we cannot provide services such as a shopping cart or e-billing.

2. Performance Cookies

- These cookies can be either session or persistent and their use is limited to improving the functionality of the Site. They collect information about how visitors use the Site, including web analytics, error handling and testing of various page structures.

These cookies do not collect information that can identify you, as all data collected is anonymous and is only used to improve the Site.

3. Functionality cookies

- These cookies can also be session or persistent and usually arise as a result of user actions. They can remember settings applied to the Site (e.g., layout, font size, color preferences) and prevent you from being asked to fill out surveys again.

Functional cookies can provide information to access additional services (e.g., offering live chat) and fulfill user requests (e.g., submitting comments).

Profiling cookies

Third-party profiling cookies for marketing / retargeting purposes

These cookies allow our partners to track your browsing behavior and interests in order to show you targeted advertisements and personalized content. By consenting to the use of these cookies, you are allowing our partners to create profiles based on your online activity to better tailor their marketing efforts to you. These cookies may also collect information about your interactions on our website for statistical analysis purposes. Additionally, they may integrate features from popular social media platforms to enhance your browsing experience.

How do I disable cookies?

We remind you that, in accordance with current legislation on the use of cookies, your prior consent is not required for technical cookies. However, if you do not consent to the use of any cookie, you must adjust your browser settings and disable cookies or stop using the site. However, by disabling cookies, the site or some of its features may not function properly.

To change the use of cookies and block/delete them on your device, simply go into your browser settings. Cookie settings may differ with each browser, although the steps are very similar. For more information on the procedure, visit http://www.aboutcookies.org or view the Help section of your specific browser.

To learn more about cookies and manage your preferences for third-party cookies, visit http://www.youronlinechoices.com”
